AKAN DAY NAMES.

This app equips us with tools to calculate the original day we were born.

It also shows us what our Akan names are.

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.

In Ghana,it is a culture to them to name new born babies using Akan names.

There are specific names to both male and female.

FAMOUS GHANIANS WITH AKAN NAMES.

  1. Kwame Nkrumah-The former President of Ghana.

    2. Kwame means he was born on a Saturday.

  2. Kofi Atta Annan-seventh Secretary Genaral of the United Nations.

    3. Kofi means he was a male born on Friday.

    2. Abena-female born on Tuesday.
